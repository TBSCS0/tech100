100 Years of Technology

By Class 8B

This website is the final product of 2 lessons of research made by the class 8B. The students had to undertake research and develop a website about the evolution of technology in the last 100 years.

They elected 7 different topics and created the following websites....

  • Alan Turing and Cryptography
  • Adm. Grace Hopper and Programming Languages
  • Linus Torvalds and Operating Systems
  • Apple and IBM Computers
  • Apple and Blackberry Phones
  • Social Networks
  • Spotify and Netflix
